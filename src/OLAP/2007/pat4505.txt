A method of producing on-demand wallpaper for sale to a consumer, the method including the steps of: providing a printing system for producing wallpaper comprising: at least one media cartridge containing a blank media web; a printhead extending at least the width of the media web; at least one processor to control printing by the printhead of a wallpaper pattern on the blank media web to form the wallpaper; an input device in communication with the at least one processor; and, a slitter module to cut the media web to a selected width; receiving, from the consumer via the input device, data indicating the consumer selected wallpaper pattern and width; printing the selected wallpaper pattern on the blank media web; cutting the printed wallpaper according to the consumer selected width; and, charging the consumer for the wallpaper.
