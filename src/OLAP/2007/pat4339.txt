A robotic system that includes a remote controlled robot. The robot may include a camera, a monitor and a holonomic platform all attached to a robot housing. The robot may be controlled by a remote control station that also has a camera and a monitor. The remote control station may be linked to a base station that is wirelessly coupled to the robot. The cameras and monitors allow a care giver at the remote location to monitor and care for a patient through the robot. The holonomic platform allows the robot to move about a home or facility to locate and/or follow a patient.
Claims What is claimed is: 1. A method for monitoring a patient within their home with a tele-presence robot, comprising: providing a mobile robot in the patient's home, the mobile robot being controlled from a remote station; capturing an image of an operator operating the remote station; transmitting the image of the operator to the mobile robot; displaying the image of the operator on a monitor of the mobile robot; transmitting a command from the remote station to move the mobile robot; moving the mobile robot within the patient's home in response to the command; capturing an image of the patient with a robot camera; and, transmitting the patient's image to the remote station. 2. The method of claim 1, wherein the image of the operator is a video image. 3. The method of claim 1, wherein a sound generated by the operator is transmitted from the remote station and produced by the robot. 4. The method of claim 1, wherein the image of the operator is transmitted through a broadband network. 5. The method of claim 4, wherein the command is transmitted through the broadband network. 6. The method of claim 1, further comprising displaying on the robot monitor an image stored in a mass storage device of the robot. 7. The method of claim 1, wherein the operator is a doctor. 8. The method of claim 1, wherein the operator is a caregiver. 9. The method of claim 1, wherein the operator is a family member.
