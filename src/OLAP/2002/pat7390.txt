Compounds, compositions and methods are provided for inhibiting the expression of human Her-1. The compositions comprise oligonucleotides complementary to mRNA targeted to nucleic acids encoding Her-1. Methods of using these oligonucleotides for inhibition of Her-1 expression and for treatment of diseases such as cancers associated with overexpression of Her-1 are provided.
