Antisense compounds, compositions and methods are provided for modulating the expression of SR-cyp. The compositions comprise antisense compounds, particularly antisense oligonucleotides, targeted to nucleic acids encoding SR-cyp. Methods of using these compounds for modulation of SR-cyp expression and for treatment of diseases associated with expression of SR-cyp are provided.
