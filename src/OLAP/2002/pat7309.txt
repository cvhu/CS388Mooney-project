Antisense compounds, compositions and methods are provided for modulating the expression of Protein Phosphatase 2 catalytic subunit alpha. The compositions comprise antisense compounds, particularly antisense oligonucleotides, targeted to nucleic acids encoding Protein Phosphatase 2 catalytic subunit alpha. Methods of using these compounds for modulation of Protein Phosphatase 2 catalytic subunit alpha expression and for treatment of diseases associated with expression of Protein Phosphatase 2 catalytic subunit alpha are provided.
