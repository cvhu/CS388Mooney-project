Antisense compounds, compositions and methods are provided for modulating the expression of calreticulin. The compositions comprise antisense compounds, particularly antisense oligonucleotides, targeted to nucleic acids encoding calreticulin. Methods of using these compounds for modulation of calreticulin expression and for treatment of diseases associated with expression of calreticulin are provided.
