Antisense compounds, compositions and methods are provided for modulating the expression of PTPN2. The compositions comprise antisense compounds, particularly antisense oligonucleotides, targeted to nucleic acids encoding PTPN2. Methods of using these compounds for modulation of PTPN2 expression and for treatment of diseases associated with expression of PTPN2 are provided.
