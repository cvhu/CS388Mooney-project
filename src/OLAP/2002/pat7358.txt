Antisense compounds, compositions and methods are provided for modulating the expression of CHK2. The compositions comprise antisense compounds, particularly antisense oligonucleotides, targeted to nucleic acids encoding CHK2. Methods of using these compounds for modulation of CHK2 expression and for treatment of diseases associated with expression of CHK2 are provided.
