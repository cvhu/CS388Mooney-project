Antisense compounds, compositions and methods are provided for modulating the expression of A20. The compositions comprise antisense compounds, particularly antisense oligonucleotides, targeted to nucleic acids encoding A20. Methods of using these compounds for modulation of A20 expression and for treatment of diseases associated with expression of A20 are provided.
