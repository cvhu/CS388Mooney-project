The present invention pertains to an apparatus for making grape leaves with meat. The apparatus comprises a mechanism for rolling a grape leaf about the meat. Additionally, the apparatus comprises a mechanism for positioning the grape leaf and meat so the rolling mechanism can roll the grape leaf about the meat.
Claims What is claimed is: 1. A method of making a grape leaf with meat comprising the steps of: placing a grape leaf on a conveyor belt; positioning a quantity of meat on said grape leaf; transporting the grape leaf and meat to a rolling station; rolling the grape leaf about the meat to form a rolled product; and packaging the rolled product.
