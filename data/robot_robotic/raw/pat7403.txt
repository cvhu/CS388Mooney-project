Antisense compounds, compositions and methods are provided for modulating the expression of Casein kinase 2-beta. The compositions comprise antisense compounds, particularly antisense oligonucleotides, targeted to nucleic acids encoding Casein kinase 2-beta. Methods of using these compounds for modulation of Casein kinase 2-beta expression and for treatment of diseases associated with expression of Casein kinase 2-beta are provided.
