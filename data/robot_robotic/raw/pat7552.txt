Antisense compounds, compositions and methods are provided for modulating the expression of RECQL2. The compositions comprise antisense compounds, particularly antisense oligonucleotides, targeted to nucleic acids encoding RECQL2. Methods of using these compounds for modulation of RECQL2 expression and for treatment of diseases associated with expression of RECQL2 are provided.
