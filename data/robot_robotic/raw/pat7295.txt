Antisense compounds, compositions and methods are provided for modulating the expression of bifunctional apoptosis regulator. The compositions comprise antisense compounds, particularly antisense oligonucleotides, targeted to nucleic acids encoding bifunctional apoptosis regulator. Methods of using these compounds for modulation of bifunctional apoptosis regulator expression and for treatment of diseases associated with expression of bifunctional apoptosis regulator are provided.
