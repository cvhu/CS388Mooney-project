The present invention provides methods for diagnosing mental disorders. The invention also provides methods of identifying modulators of mental disorders as well as methods of using these modulators to treat patients suffering from mental disorders.
Claims What is claimed is: 1. A method of decreasing anxiety-like behavior in a subject diagnosed with a mood disorder comprising administering to the subject a therapeutically effective amount of FGF2 that is sufficient to decrease anxiety-like behavior in the subject, wherein said FGF2 is administered orally, intravenously, intrathecally, or intraperitoneally. 2. The method of claim 1, wherein the mood disorder is major depression disorder. 3. The method of claim 1 or 2, wherein said therapeutically effective amount is from 1 nanogram to 10 milligrams per kilogram of said subject's body weight. 4. The method of claim 1, wherein said FGF2 is administered with a pharmaceutically acceptable carrier. 5. The method of claim 1, wherein said subject is a human. 6. The method of claim 1, wherein said FGF2 is a recombinant polypeptide.
